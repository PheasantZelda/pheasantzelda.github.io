What is "SmashAna"?

　SmashAna is a statistics website that compiles win rates and tier rankings based on match results from "Smashmate*", an online matchmaking platform for Super Smash Bros. Ultimate. While many community members create tier lists and matchup charts, this site focuses on quantitative analysis based on actual win/loss data from online matches. All detailed statistics are available for you to explore. As of January 2026, we have compiled approximately 12.6 million matches from Seasons 15-33 (including sub-seasons). Data is updated after each season ends, so please check back regularly.
　*Smashmate is an online platform operated by coco (formerly Nochou) where players can easily find matches, track ratings, and host tournaments.
